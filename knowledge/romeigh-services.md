## DENR Regulatory Framework

### Wastewater Discharge Standards
Under DAO 2021-08 and RA 9275 (Clean Water Act):
- Effluent must meet Class C water quality standards before discharge
- BOD limit: 50 mg/L for most industries
- TSS limit: 70 mg/L
- pH range: 6.0-9.0
- Heavy metals must meet specific limits
- Dischargers must obtain a Discharge Permit from DENR-EMB

### Air Emissions
Under RA 8749 (Clean Air Act) and DAO 2000-81:
- Boiler and combustion facilities need a Permit to Operate
- Emission testing required every two years
- Pollution Control Officers (PCOs) must be registered with DENR-EMB

## Common Compliance Questions

**Q: Do I need a Pollution Control Officer (PCO)?**
A: Yes, if your facility has a boiler, wastewater treatment plant, or generates significant pollution, Philippine regulations require a registered PCO. Contact ROMEIGH to discuss your compliance needs.

**Q: How often do I need to submit compliance reports?**
A: Self-Monitoring Reports (SMR) are typically quarterly. Annual Compliance Reports are submitted to DENR-EMB by March 31 each year.
